About Joie

Joie was founded with the intention of bringing together passionate Austin dancers to bring joy through movement to our community.

Company Member Requirements

Prerequisite Experience

  • 5-10 Years of Professional Dance Experience
  • 2-5 Years of Dance Teaching Experience

Personal Commitments

  1. Give 100% Effort, While Appreciating Your Own Limitations
  2. Communicate Openly and Honestly with Your Fellow Company Members
  3. Keep Your Physicality up to Par
