Girls' Haven

Turning Tragedy into Triumph

HOW CAN I HELP?

Girls' Haven offers more than just a shelter.

As a General Residential Facility, we meet more than just basic needs. We provide a loving home where girls can work through past trauma and harm to learn to make healthy decisions and choose a positive future. At Girls' Haven we believe each young lady we are entrusted with has the opportunity to turn Tragedy into Triumph!

Ehrhart School

On-Campus Charter School can be attended by Girls' Haven girls.

Home Life

We have a homelike environment with 2 beautiful living areas, a pool, gym, dance room and beautifully furnished bedrooms.
